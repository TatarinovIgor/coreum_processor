About Idea


Coreum Processing is a tool that helps businesses to establish business processes on the base of Coreum. Our service has all the required functions to manage merchants and a simple-to-use API that will allow businesses to integrate faster with minimal effort.

Services



Payments Processing


Accept payments and withdraw in Coreum blockchain


User Management


Manage your merchants and their access to the service


Assets Management


Issue, mint and burn assets on Coreum blockchain

Use Cases



Coreum based CRM


Coreum-based CRM would provide a list of unique functions to our merchants to establish financial flow and provides integrated pay-in and payouts mechanism in Coreum.

KYC Protocol


Using our processor we can implement a KYC procedure. We would create a flow that would confirm the identity of the holder of the token and allow the holder to access certain functions.

B2B transfer protocol


For every business, we would be issuing unique tokens which they can later on exchange with other businesses. Then this tokens will be exchanged between businesses.
